Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

FractureOrSprain? collects the following types of information:

  • Health Information: Symptom data, injury details, pain levels, and mobility assessments you provide through our questionnaire
  • Medical Images: X-ray images you voluntarily upload for AI analysis
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our service, including pages visited and features used
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide AI-powered injury assessment and triage recommendations
  • Analyze symptoms using our machine learning models
  • Improve the accuracy and performance of our diagnostic algorithms
  • Communicate important health recommendations and next steps
  • Maintain and improve our service quality

3. Data Security & Storage

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your health information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure server infrastructure with regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal health information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular data backups and disaster recovery procedures
  • Assessment data is processed locally and not permanently stored on our servers

4. AI & Machine Learning

Our service uses artificial intelligence and machine learning models to analyze your symptoms. Your anonymized data may be used to train and improve these models, but no personally identifiable information is retained in our training datasets.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: Powers our AI Doctor chatbot for health questions
  • Analytics Services: To understand usage patterns and improve user experience

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we have about you
  • Request deletion of your personal health information
  • Opt-out of data collection for research purposes
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Data Retention

Session data is temporarily stored during your assessment and automatically cleared when you close your browser. We do not maintain long-term records of individual assessments unless you explicitly consent to save your results.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our service after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
